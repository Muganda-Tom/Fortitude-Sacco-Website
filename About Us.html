About Us

Located in Mbita, Kenya, Homa-Bay County, Fortiude Sacco has a rich history that showcases its growth and success as a major financial institution in the region. Established in the year 2000 as Suba Teachers Sacco, it has since evolved into a prominent sacco with a membership exceeding 1,000+ individuals from the local community. Under the leadership of Mr. Odhiambo Muganda, a dedicated CEO, and a 13 member Board of Directors chaired by Mr.Tom Oketch, Fortitude Sacco has demonstrated exceptional commitment to serving its members and providing them with a wide range of financial services. With its main branch in Mbita and an additional branch in Magunga, the sacco ensures convenient access to its services for all its members. We have Currently comprise of 32 staff Members working closely with the Management to run the operations of this prestigious institution.Our departments are as follows: Accounting And Finance depertment Headed by Mr.Ken Ong'ombe, ICT depertment by Mr. Sam Oluoch, FOSA by Ms. Lektar Akoth, BOSA by Mrs Grace Mwangi and finally Customer Service by Ms Nycelynne Peter.

Since its inception, Fortitude Sacco has steadily grown its membership base and diversified its product offerings to meet the changing needs of its members. It has become renowned for its dedication to customer service, transparency, and responsible financial management. The sacco prides itself on empowering its members with the knowledge and tools necessary for sound financial decision-making. Fortitude Sacco has also actively participated in the socio-economic development of the local community. It has supported various initiatives aimed at improving education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability. By collaborating with local organizations and government bodies, the sacco has made a positive impact on the lives of the people it serves. As technology continues to advance, Fortitude Sacco has embraced digital solutions to enhance the convenience and accessibility of its services. With a user-friendly online platform and mobile banking capabilities, members can easily manage their accounts, access loans, and engage with the sacco from the comfort of their homes or workplaces. Looking ahead, Fortitude Sacco remains committed to its mission of promoting financial well-being and economic growth in the region. By continuing to serve its members with dedication and integrity, the sacco aims to solidify its position as a trusted financial partner. With its visionary leadership, strong member base, and commitment to community development, Fortitude Sacco is poised to make significant contributions to the prosperity of Mbita and its surrounding areas for years to come.

Our Mission

To empower our members and the community with financial solutions that foster stability and prosperity. We are committed to providing accessible and trustworthy financial services that meet the diverse needs of our members, while promoting financial literacy and sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading financial institution, known for our exceptional member-centric approach and innovative solutions. We strive to be a trusted partner in our members' financial journey, consistently delivering personalized services that enhance their financial well-being and contribute to the overall development of the community we serve.
