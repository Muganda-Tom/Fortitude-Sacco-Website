About Us

Located in Mbita, Kenya, Homa-Bay County, Foriude Sacco has a rich history that showcases its growth and success as a major financial institution in the region. Established in the year 2000 as Suba Teachers Sacco, it has since evolved into a prominent sacco with a membership exceeding 1,000 individuals from the local community.
