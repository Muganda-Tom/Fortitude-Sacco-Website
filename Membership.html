Fortitude Sacco - FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

Q: What is Fortitude Sacco?
A: Fortitude Sacco is a cooperative society that provides a range of financial services, including savings, loans, and investment opportunities, to its members.
Q: How can I become a member of Fortitude Sacco?
A: To become a member, you need to meet the eligibility criteria and complete the membership application process. Please visit our Membership page for more information.
Q: What types of loans does Fortitude Sacco offer?
A: Fortitude Sacco offers a variety of loan products, including personal loans, business loans, and asset financing. Our loans come with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options.
Q: How can I check my account balance?
A: You can check your account balance by logging into your online banking account or by visiting one of our branches and requesting assistance from our customer service representatives.
Q: What are the benefits of saving with Fortitude Sacco?
A: Saving with Fortitude Sacco allows you to earn competitive interest on your savings, gain access to affordable loans, and become part of a community that prioritizes financial empowerment and support.
Q: How can I contact Fortitude Sacco for further assistance?
A: You can reach our customer support team by phone at +254717199744 or by email at info@fortitude.co.ke . We are available to assist you during our business hours from Monday to Friday.

About Us

Located in Mbita, Kenya, Homa-Bay County, Foriude Sacco has a rich history that showcases its growth and success as a major financial institution in the region. Established in the year 2000 as Suba Teachers Sacco, it has since evolved into a prominent sacco with a membership exceeding 1,000 individuals from the local community.
